What is Tokenization?

1.Tokenization is the process of breaking down a text into smaller units called tokens. These tokens can be individual words, phrases, or even sentences, depending on the level of granularity required.


2.The purpose of tokenization is to provide a standardized way of representing text that can be easily processed by computer programs.


Introduction to Machine Learning

Machine learning is a type of artificial intelligence (AI) that involves teaching machines to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. It involves developing algorithms that can analyze and learn from data, and use that learning to make predictions or decisions. Machine learning has numerous applications in various industries, including healthcare, finance, and transportation.
